# Audience Personas

## Overseas Procurement Manager

Needs a stable supplier for custom machined components, predictable lead times, export communication, and consistent quality.

Content preferences:

- Supplier capability pages
- RFQ checklists
- Case studies
- Quality assurance proof

## Hardware Startup Founder

Needs prototype support, DFM feedback, and a path from small batch production to scalable manufacturing.

Content preferences:

- Prototype guides
- Cost-control articles
- Low-volume manufacturing content
- Practical manufacturing advice

## Mechanical or Structural Engineer

Needs material, tolerance, finishing, and process guidance before sending drawings for production.

Content preferences:

- Technical explainers
- Material comparisons
- Process selection guides
- Design-for-manufacturing notes

## Supply Chain Lead for Hardware Brands

Needs supplier reliability, product consistency, quality systems, and responsive manufacturing communication.

Content preferences:

- Capability overviews
- Industry applications
- Quality and delivery messaging
- Supplier comparison content
