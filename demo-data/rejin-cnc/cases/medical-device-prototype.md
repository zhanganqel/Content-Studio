# Medical Device Metal Parts Prototype Support

## Summary

A demo case scenario for stainless steel and aluminum medical device parts that require prototype validation, stable machining quality, and controlled finishing.

## Customer Need

The customer needs custom metal parts for a medical device project and must validate structure, material choice, finish quality, and machining feasibility before production.

## Rejin CNC Support

- Reviewed part requirements for manufacturability.
- Supported CNC machining for stainless steel and aluminum parts.
- Considered surface finishing and inspection expectations during prototype validation.
- Helped the customer prepare for a more stable production discussion after the prototype stage.

## Marketing Use

This case can support content about prototype validation, medical device metal parts, DFM support, precision machining, and custom CNC manufacturing for regulated or quality-sensitive products.
