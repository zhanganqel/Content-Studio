# Custom Metal Parts Sourcing for Overseas Buyers

## Summary

A sourcing solution for overseas procurement teams that need one supplier to coordinate CNC turning, CNC milling, sheet metal fabrication, finishing, quotation review, and export communication.

## Target Scenario

Procurement teams need to reduce supplier fragmentation while keeping custom part quality, delivery communication, and quotation requirements clear.

## Useful Knowledge

- Rejin CNC service pages cover multiple manufacturing processes.
- A single supplier can simplify communication for mixed CNC machining and sheet metal requirements.
- Clear RFQ details should include drawings, material, quantity, tolerance, finishing, and inspection needs.
- Export-oriented communication is important for repeat orders and delivery planning.
