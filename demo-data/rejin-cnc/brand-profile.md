# Brand Profile

## Type

Brand profile

## Summary

Rejin CNC is a China-based precision manufacturing supplier focused on custom CNC machining, sheet metal fabrication, surface finishing, and DFM support for overseas industrial customers.

## Positioning

A reliable precision machining partner for global hardware brands, engineering teams, purchasing managers, and startups that need high-quality custom metal components.

## Core Capabilities

- CNC turning
- CNC milling
- 5-axis CNC machining
- Sheet metal fabrication
- Surface treatments
- DFM support
- Prototype and low-volume manufacturing
- Custom precision metal parts

## Brand Style

- Professional
- Engineering-focused
- Precise
- Practical
- Trustworthy
- B2B and quote-oriented

## Messaging Principles

- Lead with manufacturing capability and quality control.
- Use concrete applications, materials, tolerances, and industry scenarios.
- Keep claims credible and export-market focused.
- Make calls to action direct and suitable for RFQ conversion.

## Certifications and Proof Points

- ISO 9001:2015
- IATF 16949
- Public service coverage includes CNC turning, CNC milling, 5-axis machining, sheet metal fabrication, surface treatments, and DFM support.
