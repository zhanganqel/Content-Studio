# What certifications do GOWE products have?
- ID: certifications
- Type: faq
- Tags: certification, ISO 9001, EN 12811
- Source: https://www.gowe-group.com/support/
- Answer: GOWE cites ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, EN 1090-2, and EN 12811 certification coverage for quality, environmental, occupational safety, steel structure, and ringlock scaffolding systems.
## Summary
GOWE cites ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, EN 1090-2, and EN 12811 certification coverage for quality, environmental, occupational safety, steel structure, and ringlock scaffolding systems.
